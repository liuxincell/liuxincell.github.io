Xin Liu

I am a final year PhD student in Electrical Engineering at Arizona State University, advised by Prof. Lei Ying. I received my Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering at Hunan University in 2011, and Master's degree in Signal and Information Processing at University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2014.

Research

I'm interested in resource optimization in stochastic networks and performance analysis in large-scale server systems. I also worked on some interesting problems (e.g. TSP) in Operation Research when I did intern at ShanShu, and recently on reinforcement learning (e.g. in scheduling).
